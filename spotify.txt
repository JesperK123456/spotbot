Hoe roep ik een methode aan in spotifyy.SpotifyExtension vanuit AIML:
    <extension path="spotifyy.spotifyy.SpotifyExtension">
        [naam van de def die gerunned moet worden] (, [parameter value])*
    </extension>


Alle *-FAIL S: <pattern []-FAIL ^> met optionele error message

LOGINFIRST

login(username), returns: LOGIN-OK, LOGIN-FAIL
isloggedin(), returns: LOGINFIRST, LOGIN-OK

play(), pause(), stop(), next_track(), prev_track(), returns: PLAYB-OK, PLAYB-FAIL

shuffle(), returns: SHUFFLE-OFF, SHUFFLE-ON
repeat(), returns: REPEAT-ON, REPEAT-OFF

add_curr_to_fav(), returns: ADD-OK, ADD-FAIL + [songname]
curr_on_saved(), returns: ONSAVED-YES, ONSAVED-NO, ONSAVED-FAIL + [error]

find(query, kind, offset, limit, play),  returns: PLAY-OK [song], PLAY-FAIL
                                                FIND-OK [results], FIND-FAIL, FIND-FAIL-NORESULTS
